# Product Vision

## Product Identity

Dovetails FSM is a residential handyman and home maintenance operating system focused on preserving property history, managing client relationships, creating accurate estimates, executing work efficiently, and maintaining a permanent service record for every property.

## What The Product Is

Dovetails FSM runs the Dovetails Services operating workflow from first request through paid invoice and permanent property history. It is built for a small residential service business that needs calm, reliable tools for office coordination, field execution, pricing, billing, and long-term client trust.

The product center is the relationship between a client and a property. Jobs, visits, estimates, invoices, photos, notes, and completion records should accumulate into a useful service history for the home.

## Core Outcomes

- Capture new client and property requests without creating duplicate or ambiguous work.
- Maintain a clear property record that preserves service history and field observations.
- Produce accurate estimates with pricing guardrails and visible review state.
- Schedule and execute visits with the information technicians need on site.
- Convert completed work into invoices and payment history.
- Keep every workflow step traceable without multiplying dashboards or product concepts.

## Primary Users

- Owner/Admin: reviews intake, prices work, schedules visits, invoices clients, and manages business risk.
- Office/Admin: keeps clients, estimates, invoices, and follow-ups moving.
- Technician: sees assigned visits, executes work, records notes/materials/photos, and completes visit documentation.

## Product Boundaries

These are not current product identity:

- A generic multi-company field service platform.
- A SaaS subscription platform.
- A membership-first business system.
- A dashboard suite.
- An AI estimating product.

Memberships, automations, AI assistance, dashboards, and portals may exist as features or future support, but they do not define the product direction.
