# Roadmap

## Current Priority

Reduce product identity drift and strengthen the core residential handyman operating workflow:

```text
Client -> Property -> Estimate -> Job -> Visit -> Invoice -> History
```

## Phase 1: Documentation Canonicalization

Status: In progress

- Create canonical documentation.
- Move old strategy and phase documents out of active instruction paths.
- Update root guidance to reference canonical product direction only.
- Preserve historical material in archive/generated folders.

## Phase 2: Property-Centered Workflow

Status: Next

- Make property history easy to find from client, job, visit, estimate, and invoice surfaces.
- Keep service history tied to the property rather than scattered across workflow pages.
- Promote useful visit evidence into the permanent property record.

## Phase 3: Estimate And Execution Clarity

Status: Next

- Keep estimate guardrails visible and enforceable.
- Make the handoff from approved estimate to job readiness explicit.
- Keep visit execution focused on schedule, scope, notes, materials, media, and completion.

## Phase 4: Billing And History Closure

Status: Planned

- Keep invoice creation and payment state connected to completed work.
- Make completion records and billing history visible from the property.
- Reduce duplicate operational views that show the same work in different terms.

## Out Of Scope For Current Roadmap

- Advanced membership architecture.
- Subscription billing strategy.
- Multi-company SaaS scaling.
- Concierge/realtor/routing layers.
- New dashboard families.
- AI-first product repositioning.

These may be reconsidered only after the canonical workflow is stable and the current Dovetails operating model is clear.
