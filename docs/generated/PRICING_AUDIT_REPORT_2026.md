## 3. AUDIT FINDINGS & CRITICAL ADJUSTMENTS

### [!] THE "SOFT" CATEGORIES (Risk of being too cheap)
1.  **Drywall (1001-1003):** Your book treats drywall as a single labor block. Google/AI results almost always assume a **return trip** for sanding/finish. You are currently $100+ below market on 12" patches.
    *   **Fix:** Ensure the "Return Trip" modifier ($95) is added to any patch requiring more than a fast-dry "California" method.
2.  **Toilet Replacement (2005):** At $235, you are the cheapest professional in Derry. Market minimum is closer to $350 for the risk involved.
    *   **Fix:** Adjust "Standard Swap" to a **$295 floor**. This remains "fair" while covering the risk of a flange leak or valve failure.

### [OK] THE "GOLDILOCKS" CATEGORIES (Fair but Capable)
1.  **Electrical (3001-3006):** Your pricing is exactly in line with AI/Google "Fair Price" snippets. A customer googling "Cost to install ceiling fan Derry NH" will find $250-$400. Your $215-$325 range puts you right in the sweet spot.
2.  **Mounting (7001):** You are slightly more aggressive than national chains. This is a great "referral generator." It’s a job you do perfectly and quickly, making the customer feel they got a "fair deal" compared to Best Buy or Angi.
