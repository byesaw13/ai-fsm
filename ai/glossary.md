# Glossary of Terms: Dovetails FSM

This is a compact AI reference derived from docs/canonical/DOMAIN_MODEL.md and docs/canonical/WORKFLOW.md. If this file conflicts with docs/canonical, docs/canonical wins.

Use these terms as quick vocabulary reminders, not as an authoritative glossary.

- **Client**: The person, household, or organization paying for services.
- **Property**: The physical residential home or commercial service location. (The central asset of the system).
- **Estimate**: The priced proposal submitted to the client for approval.
- **Job**: The active thread of work accepted by the client.
- **Visit**: A scheduled technician execution event at a property.
- **Invoice**: The billing document issued for payment collection.
- **Payment**: Money received against a issued invoice.
- **Observation**: A finding, condition notes, or equipment status recorded by a technician on-site.
- **Timeline**: The derived, chronological history of all visits, work, invoices, and observations for a property.
