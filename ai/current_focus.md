# Current Strategic Focus Summary

This document summarizes the current strategic focus areas of Dovetails FSM. Completed tasks and bug hotfixes belong in the changelog (`docs/changelog.md`), not here.

## 1. Active Strategic Focus Areas

- **Property-Centered Workflow**: Establishing the property timeline as the central hub of truth. Ensuring completed visit logs and observations promote to the property record.
- **Sequential Job Numbering**: Implementing clean, chronological, account-scoped job numbering (e.g. Job #1001) generated deterministically upon estimate approval.
- **Estimate Generation**: Standardizing the estimate builder interface using structured price book options and room-by-room configurations.
- **Property Timeline View**: Optimizing the performance and presentation of the chronological property read-model.
- **Invoice Workflow**: Automating transition from visit completion to invoice generation.
